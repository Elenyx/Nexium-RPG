Getting Started with Nexium RPG

Welcome to the dimensional multiverse! This comprehensive guide will help you understand the basics of Nexium RPG, from your first character pull to mastering the collection system.

Table of Contents

  • Introduction
  • First Steps
  • Character Pulling
  • Collection Management
  • Energy System
  • Next Steps

Introduction

Welcome to Nexium RPG, the ultimate Discord bot for anime character collection and battles! This guide will walk you through everything you need to know to start your journey in the dimensional multiverse.

Nexium RPG combines the excitement of gacha mechanics with strategic RPG elements, allowing you to collect characters from your favorite anime series and engage in epic battles with other players.

First Steps

Before you can start collecting characters, you'll need to set up your profile. The bot automatically creates a profile for you when you use your first command.

Basic Commands

  • /profile - View your profile and statistics
  • /balance - Check your current coins and energy
  • /help - Get a list of all available commands

Character Pulling

The heart of Nexium RPG is character collection through the gacha system. You can summon characters using coins or special items.

Pull Commands

  • /pull - Pull a single character (costs 100 coins)
  • /pull10 - Pull 10 characters at once (costs 1000 coins)
  • /daily - Claim your daily free pull and coins

Collection Management

Once you start collecting characters, you'll want to manage your collection effectively.

Viewing Your Collection

  • /collection - View all your characters
  • /collection [rarity] - Filter by specific rarity
  • /character [name] - View details of a specific character

Character Progression

You can upgrade your characters using shards and coins:

  • Level Up - Increase character level using coins
  • Rarity Upgrade - Upgrade character rarity using shards
  • Star Enhancement - Enhance characters within their rarity tier

Energy System

Nexium RPG uses an energy system to prevent spam and maintain game balance:

  • You start with 10 energy points
  • Each pull costs 1 energy
  • Energy regenerates at 1 point per hour
  • Maximum energy capacity is 20 points

Next Steps

Now that you understand the basics, here are some recommended next steps:

  1. Claim your daily rewards with /daily
  2. Do your first character pull with /pull
  3. Explore your collection with /collection
  4. Learn about battles and trading in our advanced guides

Pro Tip: Save up for 10-pulls when possible, as they guarantee at least one rare or higher character!
